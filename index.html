What Lorene does

Everything in one bot.

Seven modules, all running 24/7 just for this server.

🤖
AI Chat
Talk to Lorene with ?ai, mention her directly, or let auto-starters keep the conversation going. Full context memory per conversation.
?ai ?aiclear @mention
🎣
Fishing
Cast your rod for 50 racks, reel in fish across 5 rarity tiers. Fish of the Day gives 3× sell value. Buy luck boosters to catch rarer fish more often.
?fs ?fi ?sell /fishingluck
💰
Economy — Racks
Full server economy built around racks. Earn passively by chatting, claim daily bonuses, rob other members, run heists, buy items from the shop, or blow it all on slots. Check the leaderboard to see who's running the server financially.
?racks ?daily ?rob ?heist ?lb /shop
📈
Fake Stock Market
5 in-server companies (SHADE, LORENE, CHAOS, DRIP, NOVA). Prices fluctuate live. Buy low, sell high, flex your portfolio.
?st /buystock /sellstock /portfolio
🔥
Hype System
Give or take hype from members. Use HypeNuke to blast ±10 hype for 1,000 racks. Flex your hype score on the server.
+hype -hype ?hype /hypenuke
🎰
Gambling
Coinflip, slots, dice — risk your racks for a chance to double up. Classic server degenerate activity, no judgment.
?cf ?slots ?dice
🧠
Trivia & Fun
Trivia, roasts, facts, jokes, would-you-rather, aura tracking — all the tools needed to keep chat alive and chaotic.
?trivia ?roast ?fact ?wyr ?aura

About & Changelog.

The bot behind the chaos.

Lorene was built from the ground up specifically for Shade of Thoughts. Not a multi-server public bot with generic features — this is a hand-crafted, opinionated, custom-tailored companion designed around how our community actually plays.

From the in-house stock tickers named after our in-jokes (SHADE, LORENE, CHAOS, DRIP, NOVA) to the fishing system that has members setting alarms for Fish of the Day — every module exists because someone in the server asked for it, and we built it properly.

Lorene runs 24/7 with full AI context memory, live-updating economy, and real-time stock prices. She's not just a bot — she's basically a member.

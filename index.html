What Lorene does

Everything in one bot.

Seven modules, all running 24/7 — each packed with commands and depth.

🤖
AI Chat
Talk to Lorene with ?ai, mention her directly, or let auto-starters keep the conversation going. Full context memory per conversation.
?ai ?aiclear @mention
🎣
Fishing
Cast your rod for 50 racks, reel in fish across 5 rarity tiers. Fish of the Day gives 3× sell value. Buy luck boosters to catch rarer fish more often.
?fs ?fi ?sell /fishingluck
💰
Economy — Racks
Full server economy built around racks. Earn passively by chatting, claim daily bonuses, rob other members, run heists, buy items from the shop, or blow it all on slots. Check the leaderboard to see who's running the show.
?racks ?daily ?rob ?heist ?lb /shop
📈
Fake Stock Market
5 in-server companies (SHADE, LORENE, CHAOS, DRIP, NOVA). Prices fluctuate live. Buy low, sell high, flex your portfolio.
?st /buystock /sellstock /portfolio
🔥
Hype System
Give or take hype from members. Use HypeNuke to blast ±10 hype for 1,000 racks. Flex your hype score on the server.
+hype -hype ?hype /hypenuke
🎰
Gambling
Coinflip, slots, dice — risk your racks for a chance to double up. Classic server degenerate activity, no judgment.
?cf ?slots ?dice
🧠
Trivia & Fun
Trivia, roasts, facts, jokes, would-you-rather, aura tracking — all the tools needed to keep chat alive and chaotic.
?trivia ?roast ?fact ?wyr ?aura

The bot behind the chaos.

Lorene is a hand-crafted, opinionated Discord bot built from the ground up. Not a generic multi-server bot — every module exists because someone asked for it, and we built it properly.

From the in-house stock tickers (SHADE, LORENE, CHAOS, DRIP, NOVA) to the fishing system that has members setting alarms for Fish of the Day — every feature is designed with depth and personality.

Lorene runs 24/7 with full AI context memory, live-updating economy, and real-time stock prices. She's not just a bot — she's basically part of the server.
